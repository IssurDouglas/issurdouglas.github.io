English Proficiency Quiz

Section 1: Grammar

1. Fill in the blank with the correct form of the verb:

She _____ (go) to school every day.

a) go
b) goes
c) going

2. Choose the correct sentence:

a) She have a dog.
b) She has a dog.
c) She has a dogs.

3. Which sentence is correct?

a) I no have any money.
b) I haven't any money.
c) I don't have any money.

b) Europe
